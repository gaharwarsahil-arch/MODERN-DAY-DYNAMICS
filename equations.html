1. Safety Equation

Safety = (Predictability + Responsiveness + Repair) / Threat Reactivity

Low safety predicts anxious texting spirals and protest behavior.

2. Trust Velocity

Trust Velocity = (Kept Promises x Transparency x Consistency) - Violations

Shows whether trust is compounding or entering deficit.

3. PRB

PRB = Mutual Initiation / One-Sided Pursuit

Below 1.0 over time indicates over-pursuit strain.

4. Conflict Value

Outcome Value = (Empathy + Strategy + Follow-through) / Defensiveness

Tracks whether conflict creates damage or clarity.

5. Attachment Stabilization

Stability = (Self-Regulation + Boundary Clarity + Secure Bids) / Reassurance Compulsions

Measures progress from anxious loops toward secure functioning.

6. Repair Half-Life

Repair Half-Life = Time to Baseline Connection After Conflict

Shorter half-life means stronger system resilience.
